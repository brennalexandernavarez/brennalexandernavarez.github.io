  • Ataiza's Upholstery E-Commerce Web App

  • Completed as my capstone project for my bachelor's degree in 2024, Ataiza's Upholstery Web App is a dynamic platform built using Python Django. It showcases my commitment to leveraging technical expertise and creative design to address real-world challenges effectively.

    The app provides a seamless and user-friendly experience for browsing and purchasing custom upholstery. It features a sleek and responsive design that adapts effortlessly to any device, ensuring accessibility and convenience for users.

    A standout feature of the platform is its upholstery customization tool, which allows users to personalize their selections by choosing material types, foam options, colors, and sizes to suit their specific preferences perfectly.
